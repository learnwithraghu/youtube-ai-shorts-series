# 01 — Why Demo Quality Is Not Product Quality

## Purpose
Show the gap between a polished demo and a reliable customer-facing system.

## What this chapter teaches
- A chatbot can look impressive and still be unsafe or unreliable
- Demo success usually hides missing edge cases
- Production quality requires repeatable behavior under real-world variation

## Talking points
- The model answers the happy path correctly
- The real user asks vague, urgent, or incomplete questions
- Small mistakes create real support costs
- Confidence is not correctness

## Suggested flow
1. Open with a strong demo-like example
2. Reveal where it would fail in production
3. Explain why that matters to a business
4. Transition into the shipping support scenario

## Output
Learners should leave with the idea that a good demo is only the beginning.
