# Section 1 — The Production Problem

## Goal
Make learners feel, concretely, why LLM apps fail in production and why evals are necessary.

## Section outcome
By the end of this section, learners should understand:
- why demos are not enough
- what production failures look like
- why evals belong in the product loop

## Chapters
1. `01_why_demo_quality_is_not_product_quality`
2. `02_the_shipping_support_assistant_context`
3. `03_common_production_failure_modes`
4. `04_evals_as_a_product_requirement`
5. `05_section_recap_and_bridge_to_section_2`

## Teaching intent
This section is not about implementation yet. It is about urgency, realism, and framing the problem that the rest of the course solves.
