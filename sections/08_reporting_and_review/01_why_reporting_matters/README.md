# 01 — Why Reporting Matters

## Purpose
Explain why raw evaluation scores alone are not enough.

## What this chapter teaches
- why teams need summaries
- how reporting supports decisions
- why interpretation matters as much as scoring

## Topics covered
- raw outputs vs summarized views
- communication of quality
- decision support for teams

## Talking points
- scores are useful, but they need context
- reporting helps teams act on results
- a good report makes the system easier to manage

## Output
A reporting mindset.
